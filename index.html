Introduction

Welcome to the Animal Crossing: New Horizons guide! This technical documentation will walk you through everything you need to know to master the game and unlock its full potential.

Getting Started

Objective: Begin your adventure by setting up your island.

Select your island layout.

  • Customize your character's appearance.
  • Choose a place to pitch yours and your villager's tents.
  • Meet Tom Nook and your starting villagers.
  • Unlock the Resident Services building.
Creating Your Island

Objective: Learn the ins and outs of island design and layout.

  • Choosing Your Island's Layout: This can affect the number of rivers, cliffs, and land masses at the beginning of the game before other design features are unlocked.
  • Placing Bridges and Inclines: Learn how to place bridges, inclines, and paths to connect different parts of your island.
  • Creating a Farming Area: Designate space for crop-growing and vegetable production.
Villager Interaction

Objective: Building relationships with your villagers is key.

Tips for Building Relationships:

  • Gift Giving: Give your villagers gifts to increase friendship levels.
  • Happy Hours: Spend time talking to villagers and helping with their requests.
  • Birthday Parties: Don't forget to attend villager birthday parties for bonus rewards.
  • Moving Villagers: Once friendship reaches a high level, you may be asked to help a villager move out.

List Section 1: Best Villagers for Island Design

  1. Julian: For a celestial aesthetic.
  2. Shino: For a japanese home aesthetic.
  3. Ketchup: For a cottage-core picnic aesthetic.
  4. Zucker: For a japanese eatery/takoyaki stall aesthetic

Crafting & DIY Projects

Objective: Master the art of crafting to enhance your island.

How to Craft:

  • DIY Recipes: Collect DIY recipes through bottle messages or gifts.
  • Materials: Gather materials like wood, stone, and iron to craft various furniture and tools.
  • Workbench Usage: Use a workbench to craft items.
  • Tool Customization: Customize tools to create different versions with colors and patterns.

List Section 2: Must-Have Crafting Tools

  1. Shovel: Dig holes and uncover fossils.
  2. Fishing Rod: Catch a variety of fish.
  3. Axe: Chop wood and break rocks.
  4. Vaulting Pole: Useful to jump accross rivers when a bridge is not available.

Fishing

Objective: Catch all available fish species on your island.

Fishing Tips:

  • Bait Crafting: Craft fish bait to lure specific fish species to your location.
  • Fishing Locations: Some fish spawn in specific weather conditions or times of day.
  • Fishing Rods: Upgrade your fishing rods for longer-lasting tools.

List Section 3: Tips for Catching Rare Fish

  1. Fish early in the morning (5 AM - 9 AM).
  2. Use fish bait for specific fish species.
  3. Look for rare fish at the river mouth or at the ocean.
  4. Collect specific fish during rainy or cloudy weather.

Bug Catching

Objective: Become an expert bug catcher to complete your collection.

Bug Catching Tips:

  • Time of Day: Bugs appear at different times of day, so be patient and explore throughout the day.
  • Net Usage: Use your bug net to catch flying insects like butterflies or fireflies.
  • Bug Spawn Locations: Look for bugs on flowers, trees, and rocks.
Fossils & Artifacts

Objective: Dig up fossils to add to the museum's collection.

Fossil Digging:

  1. Shovel Usage: Use your shovel to dig up buried fossils and artifacts.
  2. Fossil Identification: Take fossils to Blathers at the museum for identification.
  3. Fossil Donations: Donate identified fossils to the museum for display.
Museum Collections

Objective: Complete the museum collection by filling all categories.

How to Complete the Museum:

  1. Fish & Bugs: Keep catching fish and bugs to donate.
  2. Fossils: Uncover and identify fossils for Blathers.
  3. Art & Sculptures: Donate rare art pieces obtained from Redd.
Nook Miles & Rewards

Objective: Maximize your Nook Miles rewards to enhance your island.

How to Earn Nook Miles:

  • Daily Tasks: Complete daily challenges for Nook Miles.
  • Special Tasks: Take part in seasonal events or challenges for extra miles.
  • Redeem Rewards: Use Nook Miles to buy exclusive items, DIY recipes, and more.
Seasonal Events

Objective: Participate in seasonal events to collect limited-time items.

List of Seasonal Events:

  1. Spring Fishing Tourney: Compete in a fishing tournament each spring.
  2. Halloween: Collect spooky items and trick-or-treat with villagers.
  3. Winter Toy Day: Celebrate the holidays with festive decorations.
  4. Nature Day: Complete environmental tasks to earn rewards.
  5. Nature Festival: Participate in special events celebrating nature.

List Section 4: Seasonal Decorations

  1. Autumn Leaves: Scatter colorful leaves for fall.
  2. Snowman Decorations: Create a snowman during winter.
  3. Bamboo and Cherry Blossoms: Set up for spring and summer.

Multiplayer Features

Objective: Enjoy the game with friends through online and local multiplayer.

Multiplayer Options:

  1. Local Co-Op: Play with friends locally by visiting their islands.
  2. Online Visits: Use Dodo Airlines to visit your friends’ islands worldwide.
  3. Trading Items: Swap items and gifts with other players online.
Advanced Tips

Objective: Advanced tips for experienced players looking to maximize their experience.

Advanced Tips:

  1. Maximizing Nook Miles: Use Nook Miles to buy a variety of rewards to speed up progression.
  2. Villager Optimization: Move in high-demand villagers with specific traits.
  3. Farming Optimization: Designate spaces for growing multiple crops for efficient harvesting.

List Section 5: Key Island Locations to Visit

  1. Nook's Cranny: The primary shop for tools and furniture.
  2. Resident Services: Customize your island and interact with the community.
  3. Able Sisters: Purchase and customize clothes.
